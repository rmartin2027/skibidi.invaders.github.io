INSTRUCTIONS

- OBJECTIVES: Your objective is to wipe out the Skibidi Invaders by shooting at them with your laser cannons.
Your goal is to score as many points as you can by shooting the invaders and prevent them from getting past you at all costs!

- INSTRUCTIONS: To MOVE LEFT AND RIGHT, use the "a" and "d" keys.
You may use this to avoid being hit by the invaders and to strategize how you play!
To FIRE at the invaders, press “ space bar “. You can't rapid fire at the enemies!!

- SCORING: Points are scored after you hit an invader (10 POINTS PER INVADER).
Your score would appear and automically update on the top left corner of your screen.

- Note that the invaders travel SIDEWAYS and when they touch the side of the screen they would move closer to you by 1 line.
Additionally, another set of invaders would respawn at a random time frame, so you better watch out!


Tips: Keep up with the invaders and wipe them out as efficiently as possible!
Look out for the projectiles the invaders are shooting!
Follow the rhythm of the invaders! And lastly, enjoy the game! BEST OF LUCK PLAYER! ( P.S we hope you don't rage quit!)
